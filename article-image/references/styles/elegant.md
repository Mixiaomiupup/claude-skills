## Visual Elements

- Refined typography, balanced compositions
- Subtle gradients, sophisticated icons, thin dividers

## Style Rules

### Do

- Balanced whitespace, refined details
- Professional hierarchy, subtle accents

### Don't

- Playful elements, bright neon colors
- Casual hand-drawn feel, overcrowded layouts

## Best For

Business content, thought leadership, corporate communication.
