# notion

Minimalist hand-drawn line art, intellectual feel.

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Background | White | #FFFFFF |
| Primary | Black | #1A1A1A |
| Secondary | Dark Gray | #4A4A4A |
| Accent 1 | Pastel Blue | #A8D4F0 |
| Accent 2 | Pastel Yellow | #F9E79F |
| Accent 3 | Pastel Pink | #FADBD8 |

## Visual Elements

- Simple line doodles, hand-drawn wobble, basic shapes, stick figures
- Single-weight lines, maximum whitespace
- Pastel accents used sparingly

## Style Rules

### Do

- Maximum whitespace, simple illustrations
- Slight wobble on all lines
- Pastel accents sparingly

### Don't

- Complex illustrations, many colors
- Detailed textures, overcrowd

## Best For

Knowledge sharing, SaaS, productivity, concept explanations.
