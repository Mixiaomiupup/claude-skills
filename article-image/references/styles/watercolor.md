# watercolor

Soft artistic with natural warmth.

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary | Soft Blues | #63B3ED, #90CDF4 |
| Secondary | Warm Pinks | #FBB6CE, #F687B3 |
| Tertiary | Muted Greens | #9AE6B4, #68D391 |
| Background | Paper White | #FFFFF5 |

## Visual Elements

- Brush strokes, color bleeds, soft edges
- Paper texture, organic shapes, wet-on-wet effects

## Style Rules

### Do

- Soft edges, color bleeding, organic flow
- Natural texture, painterly feel

### Don't

- Sharp lines, geometric precision
- Flat fills, digital look

## Best For

Lifestyle, travel, creative content, personal narratives.
