## Visual Elements

- Precise labeled diagrams, measurement scales
- Cross-references, annotation lines, figure numbering

## Style Rules

### Do

- Precise labeling, scientific notation
- Clear legends, accurate proportions

### Don't

- Artistic interpretation, decorative elements
- Stylized shapes

## Best For

Biology, chemistry, academic research, technical documentation.
