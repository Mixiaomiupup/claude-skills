# Style Definitions

## Illustration Styles (for `illustrate` mode)

### notion (Default)

Minimalist hand-drawn line art, intellectual feel.

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Background | White | #FFFFFF |
| Primary | Black | #1A1A1A |
| Secondary | Dark Gray | #4A4A4A |
| Accent 1 | Pastel Blue | #A8D4F0 |
| Accent 2 | Pastel Yellow | #F9E79F |
| Accent 3 | Pastel Pink | #FADBD8 |

**Elements**: Simple line doodles, hand-drawn wobble, basic shapes, stick figures, single-weight lines, maximum whitespace.
**Do**: Maximum whitespace, simple illustrations, slight wobble, pastel accents sparingly.
**Don't**: Complex illustrations, many colors, detailed textures, overcrowd.
**Best For**: Knowledge sharing, SaaS, productivity, concept explanations.

---

### blueprint

Precise technical drawing with engineering aesthetics.

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Background | Blueprint Paper | #FAF8F5 |
| Grid | Light Gray | #E5E5E5 |
| Primary Accent | Engineering Blue | #2563EB |
| Secondary | Navy Blue | #1E3A5F |
| Tertiary | Light Blue | #BFDBFE |
| Warning | Amber | #F59E0B |
| Text | Deep Slate | #334155 |

**Elements**: Precise lines with consistent stroke weights, technical schematics, 90-degree connections, dimension lines, isometric projections, minimal charts.
**Do**: Consistent line weights, grid alignment, restrained palette, geometric precision.
**Don't**: Hand-drawn shapes, decorative flourishes, curved connections, photographic elements.
**Best For**: System design, architecture, data analysis, infrastructure, process flows.

---

### warm

Friendly, approachable, human-centered.

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary 1 | Warm Orange | #ED8936 |
| Primary 2 | Golden Yellow | #F6AD55 |
| Primary 3 | Terracotta | #C05621 |
| Background | Cream | #FFFAF0 |
| Accent 1 | Deep Brown | #744210 |
| Accent 2 | Soft Red | #E53E3E |

**Elements**: Soft gradients, rounded shapes, golden hour lighting, organic curves, friendly icons.
**Do**: Warm lighting, rounded elements, inviting atmosphere, natural textures.
**Don't**: Sharp geometric forms, cold colors, technical precision, hard edges.
**Best For**: Personal growth, lifestyle, education, storytelling, emotional content.

---

### elegant

Sophisticated, refined, professional.

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary | Soft Coral | #F687B3 |
| Secondary | Muted Teal | #4FD1C5 |
| Tertiary | Dusty Rose | #ED8796 |
| Background | Off-White | #F7FAFC |
| Text | Charcoal | #2D3748 |
| Accent | Gold | #D69E2E |

**Elements**: Refined typography, balanced compositions, subtle gradients, sophisticated icons, thin dividers.
**Do**: Balanced whitespace, refined details, professional hierarchy, subtle accents.
**Don't**: Playful elements, bright neon colors, casual hand-drawn feel, overcrowded layouts.
**Best For**: Business content, thought leadership, corporate communication.

---

### minimal

Ultra-clean, zen-like, essential.

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Background | Pure White | #FFFFFF |
| Primary | Near Black | #1A202C |
| Accent | Single color (context-dependent) | varies |

**Elements**: Single focal point, generous whitespace (60%+), essential shapes only, thin precise lines.
**Do**: Maximum negative space, single concept focus, extreme simplicity.
**Don't**: Add decoration, use multiple colors, fill empty space, add detail.
**Best For**: Philosophy, core concepts, zen, essential ideas.

---

### watercolor

Soft artistic with natural warmth.

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary | Soft Blues | #63B3ED, #90CDF4 |
| Secondary | Warm Pinks | #FBB6CE, #F687B3 |
| Tertiary | Muted Greens | #9AE6B4, #68D391 |
| Background | Paper White | #FFFFF5 |

**Elements**: Brush strokes, color bleeds, soft edges, paper texture, organic shapes, wet-on-wet effects.
**Do**: Soft edges, color bleeding, organic flow, natural texture, painterly feel.
**Don't**: Sharp lines, geometric precision, flat fills, digital look.
**Best For**: Lifestyle, travel, creative content, personal narratives.

---

### editorial

Magazine-style infographic, tech explainer.

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary | Deep Indigo | #3C366B |
| Secondary | Coral | #FC8181 |
| Background | Light Gray | #F7FAFC |
| Accent | Teal | #38B2AC |
| Text | Dark Slate | #1A202C |

**Elements**: Bold typography, data visualizations, structured sections, clear callouts, professional layout, magazine-quality composition.
**Do**: Strong hierarchy, bold headlines, data-driven visuals, clear sections.
**Don't**: Casual or hand-drawn feel, playful elements, irregular layouts.
**Best For**: Tech explainers, journalism, data stories, long-form analysis.

---

### scientific

Academic precise diagrams.

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary | Lab Blue | #2B6CB0 |
| Secondary | Research Green | #276749 |
| Tertiary | Alert Red | #C53030 |
| Background | Paper White | #FFFFF0 |
| Text | Academic Black | #1A202C |

**Elements**: Precise labeled diagrams, measurement scales, cross-references, annotation lines, figure numbering.
**Do**: Precise labeling, scientific notation, clear legends, accurate proportions.
**Don't**: Artistic interpretation, decorative elements, stylized shapes.
**Best For**: Biology, chemistry, academic research, technical documentation.

## Cover Renderings (for `cover` mode)

| Rendering | Lines | Texture | Depth | Element Style |
|-----------|-------|---------|-------|---------------|
| `flat-vector` | Uniform outlines, rounded endings | None, flat fills | Flat 2D, overlap layering | Geometric icons, bold shapes |
| `hand-drawn` | Imperfect strokes, varied weight | Paper grain, pencil | Sketch layers | Doodles, wobble, organic |
| `painterly` | Brush strokes, soft edges | Canvas, paint bleeds | Atmospheric, soft | Artistic, wet-on-wet |
| `digital` | Precise edges, anti-aliased | Subtle noise/grain | Subtle 3D, gradients | UI components, polished |
| `pixel` | Pixel grid, aliased | Dithering | None | Chunky shapes, 8-bit |
| `chalk` | Chalk strokes, dust | Blackboard grain | Layered chalk | Board doodles, erased marks |
